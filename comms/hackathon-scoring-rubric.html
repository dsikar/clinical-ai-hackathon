Scoring Scale Reference

Score Meaning
1 Not all core requirements implemented — major gaps; does not fully solve the task
2 All core requirements implemented — works end-to-end, accurate/useful Excel output, solid level
3 Surpassed expectations — noticeably better: clever features, polish, real-world ready
4 Blew my mind — exceptional: innovative, clinically transformative, wow factor

Guidance for score 4: Use sparingly for projects that genuinely surprise or impress — e.g., unusually accurate/safe extraction on tricky real-world cases, smart clinician-friendly additions (confidence flags, summaries), elegant handling of edge cases, or clear potential to save serious time/errors in NHS/MoD workflows. It should feel like "this could actually change how we work".
