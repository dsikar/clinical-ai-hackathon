Subject: Clinical AI Hackathon – Welcome and Getting Started (16–20 March)

Dear all,

Welcome to the City St George's Clinical AI Hackathon! We're delighted to have you joining us
next week.

The hackathon runs Monday 16 to Friday 20 March. The challenge is to build a tool that
extracts structured patient data from NHS MDT (multidisciplinary team) Word documents into
a searchable database format, reducing the manual burden on clinicians across the NHS.

--

GETTING STARTED

Everything you need is in the GitHub repository:

  https://github.com/dsikar/clinical-ai-hackathon

Please read the README carefully before Monday. It contains:
  - The clinical problem statement (from Dr Anita Wale)
  - The dataset: 50 synthetic MDT cases (Word doc) and an example output (Excel)
  - Success criteria
  - Technical considerations (DTAC, SaMD compliance)
