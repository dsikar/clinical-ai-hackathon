# Clinical AI Hackathon — Debrief and Invitation

We would like to invite NHS clinical staff to join us on Friday 17 April, 10:00–15:30, at Richmond American University London, Building 12, Chiswick Park, 566 Chiswick High Road, London W4 5AN.

The hackathon runs from 13–17 April 2026. Over that week, student teams work intensively on real healthcare problems, building practical software tools that could reduce workload or improve patient care. NHS clinical staff attend on the final day to review what the teams have built and share their perspective from a clinical or operational point of view.

The first hackathon focused on a colorectal cancer MDT data problem. Clinical teams currently extract structured patient data from MDT outcome documents by hand — a slow, repetitive process. Student teams built software to automate that extraction. The work was presented to a judging panel and assessed against clinical, technical, and practical criteria.

The second event continues that model. We are keen to hear about other pain points at any level — clinical, administrative, IT, or operational — that could be posed as real challenges to student teams. If there is a task in your service that is time-consuming, error-prone, or overdue for a better solution, we would welcome the conversation.
