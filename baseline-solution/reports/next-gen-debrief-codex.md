# Clinical Hackathon Debrief

We would like NHS clinical staff to join us on 17 April 2026, from 10:00 to 15:30, at Richmond American University London, Building 12, Chiswick Park, 566 Chiswick High Road, London W4 5AN. On the day, we ask staff to review what student teams have built and give feedback from a clinical or service point of view.

The hackathon runs from April 13 to 17, 2026. It brings students together for several days to work on real healthcare problems, with the aim of finding practical tools that could reduce workload or improve care.

In the first hackathon, the problem was the manual extraction of structured data from colorectal MDT outcome Word documents. Student teams built software to turn those documents into structured records. This is the kind of admin burden the event is meant to address.

We would also welcome other NHS problems that could become future student challenges, whether clinical, admin, IT, or operational.
